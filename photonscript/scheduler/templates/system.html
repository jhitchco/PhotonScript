How to capture each type
DARKS & BIAS — the button above. Prereq: roof CLOSED, night time (no shutter on the RC16 — any light ruins them). Cloudy/unsafe nights are ideal. ~3.5 h for 300s×20 + 600s×20 + 50 bias at the configured imaging gain / offset / 0°C. Refresh darks ~every 90 days.

FLATS (sky flats) — NINA Flat Wizard, run at dusk while the sun is −3° to −8° (roughly the 20 min after sunset): Wizard mode Sky flats → Multi Mode with all filters ON → Slew to zenith (East) → histogram mean 50%, tolerance 10% → 10–15 flats per filter. Re-shoot after any dust event, camera rotation, or focuser/optics change — and at least every 45 days. PhotonScript sees them automatically on the next health refresh.

Automating flats later: the Flat Wizard itself can't be scripted, but the Advanced Sequencer has a Flats instruction category (Sky Flat / Auto Exposure Flat / Trained Flat Exposure). Build a dummy advanced sequence containing those three, save it as JSON, and share the file — then dusk sky flats can be added to the scheduler automatically.
